About Me

My name is Sirawit Yuennan. I am specialized in game design, character design, 3D modelling and animations. As an aspiring video game developer, and studying in game design at Simon Fraser University's School of Interactive and Technology. Aside of studying, I'm working with a game development team as the 3D modeller and animator. Our current project is an artistic mobile game which will be planned for a winter 2019. My skill sets including knowledge in 3D modelling/animations, and game design. While my technical skills include using Unity 3D Engine, Blender, Maya and Live2D Engine. Despite having a versatile skill sets, my focus is on 3D works, especially animations.
